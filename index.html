About Us

Founded by a passionate young environmentalist, Planetearth Conserve bridges science and humanity to protect our natural world. We raise awareness, inspire connection, and empower people to heal, protect, and love our planet.

Scientific Research

We explore biodiversity in fauna and flora, from climate change and urbanization to marine ecosystems and endangered species using science to protect life on Earth.

Awareness & News

We share global news, educational content, and scientific discoveries to build awareness and encourage action for endangered species, ecosystems, and climate change.

Sustainable Activities

From eco-friendly living and marine protection to cleanup events and environmental workshops we promote sustainable actions to reconnect people with nature.
